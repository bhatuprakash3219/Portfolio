— 01 / About

A designer who codes the things he draws.

Three years of crafting user-focused digital experiences through thoughtful design and frontend development.

I'm Prakash a UI/UX Designer & Frontend Developer passionate about creating intuitive, user-centered digital experiences. I believe great design is not just about aesthetics, but about solving real user problems through thoughtful design decisions.

Over the past three years, I've worked on a variety of projects ranging from healthcare platforms and business websites to Shopify stores and web applications. My journey started at IGEX Solutions, where I built production-ready interfaces and strengthened my foundation in design and frontend development.

Today, I focus on crafting seamless user experiences using Figma, while bringing designs to life with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, and Shopify. I enjoy bridging the gap between design and development to create products that are both visually appealing and highly functional.

My goal is simple — design experiences that users love and build interfaces that businesses can trust.
